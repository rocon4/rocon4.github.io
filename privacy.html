Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Simple Calories ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS calorie counting application (the "App").

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information, calorie data, food entries, notes, or photos to any servers. Everything you enter into the App remains on your iOS device.

Information We Don't Collect

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, age, weight, height)
  • Calorie tracking data
  • Food entries and meal logs
  • Photos you take within the App
  • Notes or comments you add
  • Any other data you enter into the App

Local Data Storage

All data you enter into Simple Calories is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's standard data storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • Your calorie goals and daily targets
  • Food entries and meal history
  • Photos you capture within the App
  • Notes and comments
  • App preferences and settings

This data is only accessible on your device and is not shared with us or any third parties.

No Internet Connection Required

Simple Calories is designed to work completely unless you are using the AI camera feature. The App does not require an internet connection to function in general, and it does not send any data over the internet besides the AI camera feature which uses google gemini flash 2.5 model.

Third-Party Services

Simple Calories does not integrate with any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection services. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all App data at any time by deleting the App from your iOS device. You can also clear specific data within the App's settings.

Children's Privacy

Simple Calories does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of collecting information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the X account at https://x.com/simplecalories.
